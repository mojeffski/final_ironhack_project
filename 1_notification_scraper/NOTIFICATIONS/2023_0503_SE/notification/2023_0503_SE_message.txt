4. 2023/0503/SE - T40T - Urban and road transport

5. Provisions amending the Swedish Road Administration's Regulations (VVFS 2003:19) on cars converted to tractors and cars converted to class II motorized equipment

6. Winter tyres on tractors made from converted cars, also known as A-tractors.

7. 
 

8. The provisions complement the forthcoming amendment to Chapter 4, § 18a of the Road Traffic Ordinance (1998:1276), which introduces requirements for winter tyres on tractors made from converted cars (A-tractors). The provisions clarify the requirements for the winter tyres to be used in accordance with the Ordinance and are the same as those which already apply to cars. There is general advice that complements the regulatory provisions.

The legislative amendment has been notified to the European Commission.

9. The amendment to the Ordinance introduces a requirement for A-tractors to be using winter tyres when travelling during the period of 1 December through 31 March. Since the Ordinance does not specify which tyres are winter tyres, the purpose of the regulatory amendment is to clarify this. If these rules are not introduced, the Ordinance cannot be applied.
